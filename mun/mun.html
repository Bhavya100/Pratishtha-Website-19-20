What is a MUN ?   Model United Nations , also known MUN, is an extra-curricular activity in which students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees . MUN is considered as one of the best extra curriculars in today’s time which involves debating on specific agenda’s, public speaking , teamwork, diplomacy, leadership abilities and it gives a great exposure to the world political happenings.
