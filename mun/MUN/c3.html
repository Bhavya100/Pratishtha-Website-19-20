THE UNITED NATIONS HUMAN RIGHTS COUNCIL

DESCRIPTION


The UNHRC is a subsidiary body of the United Nations General Assembly, dealing with thematic issues relating to human rights and responsible for strengthening the promotion and protection of human rights around the globe and for addressing situations of human rights violations and make recommendations on them.

Agenda : Immigration policies around the world with special attention to Brexit and extreme measures by ICE in the USA.
