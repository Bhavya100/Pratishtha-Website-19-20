SAKEC's Marathon


Shah and Anchor college is back with it's very own SAKEC Marathon. We always have believed in Giving back to society and running for a social cause. Last year, we had our cause as "Run for the Martyred"and saw a heartwarming response and three digit registrations! This motivated us to bring a better, bigger and improved version with very important cause, "Run Against Hunger". It has been estimated that 194.4 million people in India are undernourished to eradicate hunger at our level. Change can't be brought by an individual. It is always brought by masses! Do Join us for making change. Stay tuned for updates!
