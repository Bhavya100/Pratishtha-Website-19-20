🚴🏻 SAKEC CYCLOTHON 🚴🏻‍♀

SAKEC CYCLOTHON is an annual event where we ride for a cause to create awareness. For this year, the cause is :-

*PEDAL AGAINST DEPRESSION* .

Depression,something that is frowned upon to talk or suffer through it. We wish to raise awareness for the one’s suffering through it and ride past the stigma around it.
